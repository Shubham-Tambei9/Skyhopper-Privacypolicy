This Privacy Policy explains how Sky Hopper ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our Android game.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No user registration required
  • No login system
  • No direct collection of personal information (name, email, phone number)

2. Third-Party Advertising Services

We use Unity Ads to display advertisements in Sky Hopper.

Unity Ads may automatically collect:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address (for approximate location)
  • Ad interaction data
  • App usage data

For more information, please review Unity's Privacy Policy:
https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

3. GDPR & European Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Users in the EEA will be presented with a consent request before personalized advertisements are shown. You may choose whether to allow personalized ads or non-personalized ads.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by resetting your advertising preferences in your device settings.

4. How Information Is Used

  • To display advertisements
  • To improve ad performance
  • To analyze usage trends
  • To improve game stability and fix bugs

5. Children's Privacy

Sky Hopper is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
