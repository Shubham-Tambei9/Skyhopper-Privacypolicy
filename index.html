Sky Hopper uses third-party advertising services to display ads within the game. These advertising networks may automatically collect certain information from your device for advertising and analytics purposes.

Ads shown in the game may be personalized or non-personalized depending on the user's consent, location, and device advertising settings.

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information including:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address (for approximate location)
  • Ad interaction data
  • App usage data

Google may use this information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

More information:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Unity Ads

We also use Unity Ads to display advertisements in the game.

Unity Ads may automatically collect:

Unity Privacy Policy:

https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

3. GDPR & European Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Users located in the EEA may be presented with a consent request before personalized advertisements are shown.

You may choose whether to allow personalized ads or non-personalized ads.

You can withdraw consent anytime by resetting your advertising preferences in your device settings.

4. How Information Is Used

  • To display advertisements
  • To improve advertising performance
  • To analyze usage trends
  • To improve game stability and fix bugs

5. Advertising Identifiers

Our app may use your device’s advertising identifier (such as Google's Advertising ID) to show relevant advertisements.

You can reset or limit the use of your advertising ID through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Sky Hopper is designed for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under 13.

Our app does not require user accounts and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Advertisements shown in the app are served by third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob and Unity Ads which comply with applicable privacy regulations.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

We take reasonable steps to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
