Sky Hopper

This Privacy Policy explains how Sky Hopper ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our Android mobile game.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No user registration required
  • No login system
  • No direct collection of personal information (name, email, phone number)

2. Third-Party Advertising Services

Sky Hopper uses third-party advertising networks to display advertisements in the game. These services help keep the game free to play.

The advertising networks used in this application include:

  • Google AdMob (Google Inc.)
  • Unity Ads (Unity Technologies)

These advertising partners may automatically collect certain information when ads are displayed, including:

  • Device type and device identifiers
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address (used to estimate general location)
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Ad performance data

This information is used for purposes such as:

  • Displaying relevant advertisements
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Improving advertising services

For more information about how these companies handle user data, please review their privacy policies:

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Unity Privacy Policy:
https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

3. Advertising Identifier

Sky Hopper may use the device’s advertising identifier provided by Google Play Services. This identifier allows advertising partners to provide relevant ads and measure campaign performance.

Users can reset or limit the use of their advertising identifier through their device settings.

4. GDPR & European Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Users in the EEA may be presented with a consent request before personalized advertisements are shown. You may choose to allow personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting the advertising settings on your device.

5. How Information Is Used

  • To display advertisements
  • To improve advertising performance
  • To analyze gameplay trends
  • To improve game stability and fix bugs

6. Children's Privacy

Sky Hopper is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.
